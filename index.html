Cross-Cutting Issues: Gender & Safeguarding

Structural reinforcement required in the next project cycle

Source: BA4Y Business Accelerator BDS 360-Degree Appraisal — Cross-Cutting Issues, June 2026
Gender Mainstreaming
Gap: Gender consideration was sometimes performative — acknowledged in discourse but not always operationalised in mentoring.
Action: Embed gender as an actionable BDS requirement; fund gender inclusion training.
Safeguarding Awareness
Critical Gap: No standardised reporting channels; some providers "mentioned it in passing."
Action: Establish a unified safeguarding mechanism; integrate into BDS training modules structurally.
